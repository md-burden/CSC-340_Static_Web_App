We are a group of people with a passion for googly eyes. For many years we have meticulously obsessed over finding, creating, and refining googly eyes. After all of that we decided it was time to share our passion with the world and here we are. We hope that you are just as excited about googly eyes as we are and we can't wait to see what you make!
